How to Install PlayerIQ PWA

Install directly from your browser - no app store needed. Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop.

🍎

iPhone / iPad

  1. 1
    Open in Safari Navigate to app.html in Safari browser (required for iOS)
  2. 2
    Tap Share Button Look for the share icon ⬆️ at the bottom of Safari
  3. 3
    Add to Home Screen Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" ➕
  4. 4
    Name & Add Customize the name and tap "Add"

Pro Tip: The app icon will appear on your home screen and work like a native app!

🤖

Android

  1. 1
    Open in Chrome Navigate to app.html in Chrome browser
  2. 2
    Look for Install Banner Chrome will show "Add PlayerIQ to Home screen" banner
  3. 3
    Or Use Menu Tap the three dots menu ⋮ → "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  4. 4
    Confirm Installation
